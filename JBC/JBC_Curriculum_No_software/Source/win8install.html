How to install KISS IDE on Windows 8

Step 1

Do NOT plug the KIPR Link into your computer until told to do so! Boot into Windows 8 normally.

Step 2

Move mouse into top right corner and click on the search icon, then search for “Settings” and click "Settings". Then search for “General” and click on "General Settings".

Step 3

Choose "Advanced Startup" and click “Restart Now”.

Step 4

Click “Troubleshoot”.

Step 5

Click “Advanced Options”.

Step 6

Click “Startup Settings”.

Step 7

Click “Restart”.

Step 8

Choose option 7.

Step 9

Log in to Windows 8.

Step 10

Plug in your KIPR Link via USB cable.

Step 11

Turn on your KIPR Link.

Step 12

Install this file (requires internet connection): KISS Platform 5.1.2.exe.

Step 13

Search for “Device Manager” and find “Gadget Serial v2.4”. Right click on “Gadget Serial v2.4” and choose "update driver software", then choose to search automatically. A low COM port is good (anything less than 100 should work fine).
